Flu Shot Reactions

Countless people suffer from injuries and illness following flu shot administration.  Flu shot reactions can include shoulder injuries, Guillain-Barre Syndrome, allergic reaction, brachial neuritis, and other severe reactions.

The attorneys at My Vaccine Lawyer represent many people who have suffered shoulder injuries from the flu shot.  The flu shot can cause shoulder injuries such as adhesive capsulitis, torn rotator cuff, bursitis and tendonitis. 

If you suffered a flu shot reaction, shoulder pain following a flu shot, Guillain-Barre Syndrome or any other condition following flu shot administration, please contact us today.  One of our national vaccine attorneys would be happy to provide a free consultation.  For more information regarding the type of compensation we can obtain for you, please see our vaccine injury compensation page.

What You Should Know About the Flu Shot

Flu Shot Ingredients1

The influenza vaccine contains several ingredients which may vary year to year.  The 2013-2014 flu shot contains the following ingredients:

  • inactivated influenza virus
  • Antibiotics (the type of antibiotic varies depending on vaccine manufacturer.  For instance, Sanofi Pasteur’s Fluzone® vaccine contains Neomycin while Abbot Influvac® contains Gentamicin)
  • Egg protein
  • Chicken protein
  • Formeldahyde

The 2013-2014 flu shot may contain the following ingredients:

  • CTAB
  • Triton X-100
  • Gelatin
  • Sucrose
  • Sodium deoxycholate
  • Thimerosal (mercury-based preservative)

Who should NOT get the flu vaccine?

People that suffer from severe allergies.  If you have ever had an allergic reaction after receiving the flu shot, you should avoid vaccination.

People that have had Guillain-Barre Syndrome.  Guillain-Barre Syndrome (GBS) is a severe paralyzing illness.  If you have ever been diagnosed with Guillain-Barre Syndrome you should not receive a flu shot.  If you have received a flu shot and developed Guillain-Barre Syndrome afterwards, please contact us immediately as you are likely entitled to compensation from the Vaccine Injury Compensation Program.

Flu Shot Risks

The Center for Disease Control advises that the influenza vaccine does have risks.  Flu shot side effects can include:

  • Seizures caused by fever (particularly in children)
  • Severe allergic reaction
  • Guillain-Barre Syndrome
  • Soreness, redness or swelling at the vaccination site
  • Hoarseness and cough
  • Fever
  • Aches and pains
  • Headaches
  • Itching
  • Fatigue

    If you are suffering from an allergic reaction to the flu shot, call your doctor immediately. 

Flu Shot Lawyers

The Vaccine Injury Compensation Program was created to compensate people who suffer reactions after receiving vaccines. Vaccine claims are not lawsuits against doctors, hospitals, or vaccine manufacturers. Rather, it is claim against a fund administered by the government. The vaccine manufacturers pay into this fund to compensate people that are hurt by vaccines.

If you or a loved one has been affected by a severe reaction to the flu shot, please contact My Vaccine Lawyer today and speak to one of our vaccine attorneys about your case and potential compensation.
