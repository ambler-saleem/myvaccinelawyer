We Handle Cases Across The Country

The National Vaccine Attorneys at My Vaccine Attorney are licensed to handle vaccine claims in the United States Court of Federal Claims. With that licensure, our attorneys are able to handle your vaccine case regardless of where you live. We specialize in handling vaccine cases throughout the country. With the Court located in Washington D.C., we are able to provide clients across the country with aggressive representation and handle your claim electronically. If necessary for your case, we will come visit you where you live. Most importantly, our attorneys stay in constant contact with out clients.
